Early Life and Education

Sebastian Salvatore Mulé was born on November 27, 2001, in North Catasauqua, Pennsylvania. He was born into a Catholic household with his mother having a Polish heritage and his father having a Sicilian heritage.

He attended Good Shepherd Catholic School in Northampton, PA, a private K-8 school, before attending Allentown Central Catholic High School where he would graduate in 2020. Throughout high school, he developed an interest in computer science, particularly in computer hardware and the cybersecurity field.

He attended the University of Scranton from Fall 2020 to Spring 2021 before transferring to Penn State Berks in the Fall 2021 semester where he plans to graduate. He is currently studying IT with a specialization in cyber security.

Job History

Giant Foodstores

Sebastian worked part-time as a grocery and dairy assistant at Giant Food from December 2020 to August 2021. He took on this job part-time while taking online classes at the University of Scranton and subsequently as a summer job before transferring to Penn State Berks. His position entailed stocking the shelves in the respective departments.

Abarta Coca-Cola

Sebastian's next job was as a temporary Cooler Crew member at Abarta Coca-Cola. He held this full-time position from May 2022 to August 2022, as it served as another Summer Job.This job entailed delivering, cleaning, and managing coolers that would be delivered to businesses in the Lehigh Valley Area. He also worked in delivering coolers to events like Musikfest.
